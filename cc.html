What is Class Collective?

Class collective is a community building platform that gives UChicago students more access to events going on. RSOs can pay a small fee to advertise their events, we provide sponsored events, and then people can make money by organizing their own classes.

Learn


There are many skills that aren't presented in academia, such as basic life skills or other hobbies. Class Collective offers Uchicago students the chance to learn new skills without the commitment of taking a college level course and without commiting to too many RSOs.

Such learning opportunities can include classes taught by UChicago students, events sponsored by local Hyde Park businesses, or events put on by RSOs.

Teach


Class Collective offers UChicago students the opportunity to present the skills that they have to other interested students. UChicago students can be paid to teach classes!


Contact


We also give businesses in Hyde Park an opportunity to present skills to UChicago students through sponored events. If your business would like to engage with Class Collective, please contact us.
